Some facts About Us

The Ark Herb Farm lays nestled on the side of the Barva volcano overlooking the San Jose valley. With an altitude of 3900 ft. and a year round growing season this 7.5 hectare botanical garden has a multitude of varieties of plants.

For 25 years Tommy Thomas has amassed one of the largest collections of herbs, plants, and flowers in Central America. It is a focal point for doctors, herbalists, botanists, tourists and all the plant lovers in Costa Rica.

One has the choice of an educational tour filled with anecdotes and stories or just spending a few hours strolling around the farm enjoying the view, the smells, and the beauty of nature in the Ark Herb Farmâ€™s gardens.

Plants are marked with symbols signifying their uses, such as medicinal, colorants, food, poisonous, culinary, crafts, ornamental, wood, fragrance and more.

The Ark Herb Farm also offers fresh produce and potted plants.
